Delete your Reeva account

Reeva — AI Video Generator, published by Metin Güner.

You can request deletion of your Reeva account at any time. There are two ways to do this; the in-app option is the fastest.

Option 1 — In the Reeva app (recommended)

  1. Open Reeva on your phone.
  2. Tap Settings (bottom navigation).
  3. Scroll to Account and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account is deleted immediately and the cascade runs server-side within a few seconds.
Tip: If you only signed in anonymously (skipped sign-in), there's nothing tied to a personal account — just uninstall the app and Reeva's anonymous session is gone.

Option 2 — Request by email

Email mtngnr7@gmail.com from the address you signed up with. Include:

  • The subject line: "Reeva account deletion request"
  • The email address linked to your Reeva account
  • (Optional) Reason — we use it to improve the app, not to push back on the deletion

We process email deletion requests within 30 days, usually within 1–2 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email once the deletion completes.

What gets deleted

The deletion is a full cascade across every system that holds data about your account.

Data Where Status after deletion
Email address Supabase Auth Deleted immediately
User ID + profile row Supabase Postgres (public.users) Deleted immediately
Uploaded photos Cloudflare R2 (uploads/<uid>/) Deleted immediately
Generated videos Cloudflare R2 (renders/<uid>/) Deleted immediately
Render history + credit ledger Supabase Postgres Deleted immediately (CASCADE)
Purchase / subscription record RevenueCat + public.users Deleted from our database. RevenueCat anonymizes the record per its own retention policy.
Push notification token device_tokens table Deleted immediately

What is not deleted

The following are kept for the reasons noted below:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play purchase records — the actual receipts are held by Apple / Google under their own retention policies. We can't delete those. You can request removal directly from Apple or Google.
  • Anonymous crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics) — kept up to 90 days from the original crash event. They're not linked to your user ID, so they remain anonymous.
  • Aggregated, non-identifying analytics counters (if any) — e.g. "total renders this week" used for system health monitoring. These contain no personal data.

Cancelling a subscription is separate

Important: Deleting your Reeva account does not cancel an active Apple App Store / Google Play subscription. To stop the auto-renewal, you must cancel it through the store directly. Otherwise the subscription will keep charging your Apple ID / Google account even after your Reeva account is gone.
  • iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Reeva → Cancel.
  • Android: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Reeva → Cancel.

Questions?

Write to mtngnr7@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days; usually within 1–2 business days.
