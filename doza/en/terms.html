Definition of the service

Doza is a reminder app. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or treatment recommendations. It does not use AI or algorithms to make health suggestions. Medications, vitamins, appointments and reports inside Doza are shown exactly as you enter them.

Health decisions

Consult your doctor for all health-related matters. Doza does not make decisions for you; it only triggers the reminders you set. Your physician and the medication leaflet are the only authoritative sources for doses, treatment duration, or any therapy changes.

Reliability of notifications

Whether reminders arrive on time depends on your phone's notification settings, battery optimization, "Do Not Disturb" mode and operating system behavior. Doza cannot be held responsible for missed reminders or any health consequences resulting from them. For critical doses, we recommend an additional reminder method (clock alarm, pill organizer, family member follow-up).
