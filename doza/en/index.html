About

What is Doza?

Doza is a medication and appointment reminder tool designed for people managing chronic conditions or simply busy lives. It is not a medical device; it does not diagnose or recommend treatment.

🌿
Fully on-device Your data stays on your phone. No servers, no accounts, no internet.
🔔
Timely reminders Gentle reminders for medications, vitamins, appointments and report expirations.
👨‍👩‍👧
Family profiles Track medications for your children, parents or partner in one app.
🌐
4 languages English, Turkish, German, Arabic — fully localized.

Doza is free, ad-free and tracker-free. We recommend reading the privacy policy and terms of use.
