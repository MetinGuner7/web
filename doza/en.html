What is Doza?

Doza is a medication and appointment reminder tool designed for people managing chronic conditions or simply busy lives. It is not a medical device; it does not diagnose or recommend treatment.

Privacy policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

Doza keeps your health data on your device. Nothing is sent to any server, cloud, or third party.

Data controller

Doza developer · Contact: dozaaapp@gmail.com

What data is processed?

  • Profile information: name, surname (optional), date of birth (optional), gender (optional), relationship
  • Medication and vitamin data: name, dose, frequency, instructions, start/end dates, optional notes
  • Appointment data: date, time, doctor name, department, hospital, optional notes
  • Report data: report title, validity dates, doctor, hospital, optional notes
  • Dose check-ins: record of which dose you marked as taken at which time
  • App preferences: language, theme, text size, notification on/off state

Purpose of processing

Solely to make the reminder function work: deliver notifications at the times you set and show your dose history within the app.

Legal basis

GDPR Article 6(1)(a): explicit consent. You provide consent by accepting the disclaimer during onboarding when you first open the app. The timestamp of your consent is recorded inside the app.

Retention period

Data stays on your device until you use the "Reset data" option or uninstall the app. There is no automatic deletion — you are in control.

Sharing with third parties

None. Doza does not send any data to servers, does not use third-party SDKs (no analytics, ads, or crash reporting), and does not place cookies. The Android version does not even request the android.permission.INTERNET permission (verifiable in the manifest).

International transfer

None. Since data does not leave your device, no international transfer occurs.

Your rights under GDPR Articles 15–22

You may exercise the following rights by contacting the data controller:

  • Right of access — confirmation whether your data is processed and what it contains
  • Right to rectification — correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Because Doza is a local-only app, the "Reset data" option gives you immediate, complete access to all of these rights: deletion, rectification (by re-entering), restriction, and objection.

Right to complain

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In the EU, the supervisory authority of your country of residence is competent. In Turkey, the Personal Data Protection Authority (kvkk.gov.tr).

Frequently asked questions

I'm not receiving notifications, what should I do?
The most common reason is battery optimization. In phone settings, exclude Doza from battery optimization (Android: Settings → Battery → Battery usage → Doza → Don't optimize; iOS: Settings → Doza → Notifications must be allowed + disable Low Power Mode). Also check Profile → Notifications inside the app.
On iOS, Doza is missing from the Notification Settings list.
If you did not say "Allow" during onboarding, iOS does not add the app to the list. Fix: uninstall the app, restart the phone, reinstall, and this time choose "Allow" during onboarding.
Is my data safe? Is it stored in the cloud?
Your data stays only on your phone — nothing is sent to servers, no internet access, no account creation. On Android, Doza does not even request the INTERNET permission (you can verify on the Play Store listing).
How do I transfer my data to a new phone?
Currently manual: install Doza on the new device and re-enter your medication information. This is a deliberate choice — we prevent OS-level automatic transfer of health data for GDPR/privacy reasons. Backup/sync may be added in future versions.
How do I delete my account / data?
Two ways: (1) Inside the app, go to Profile → bottom of the page → "Reset data" → confirm. (2) Uninstall Doza from your phone — all data is removed.
I want to add a profile for a family member.
Profile → My profiles → Add new profile. To hold someone else's information on their behalf, you must tick a box confirming that they have authorized you to do so.
I want to hide medication names for privacy.
When adding or editing a medication, enable the "Hide name" option. The notification will appear as "Doza is reminding you · 1 medication pending" — the actual medication name will not appear on the banner.
What is Doza+ premium?
Coming soon! In v1, all features are free. Doza+ will include extra family profiles, themes and sharing options (a one-time lifetime purchase is planned).
Bug report / suggestion
Write to dozaaapp@gmail.com. Including your device model and Doza version helps — we cannot see what's happening on your device, so details speed up diagnosis.
